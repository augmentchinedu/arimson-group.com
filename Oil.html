CRUDE OIL & REFINED PETROLEUM PRODUCTS

CRUDE OIL

We source Crude Oil from a variety of counterparties including public production companies, oil majors and national oil companies (NOCs) in Nigeria, Iraq, Russia, Oman, and Saudi Arabia Angola, and deliver to customers in Europe, USA and China. We also consult for, and work with private-sector refineries, oil companies and traders to Tender for Crude Oil Lighting Rights in national oil companies in Nigeria, Iraq, and Angola from time to time.

Refined Petroleum Products

Working with some of the finest refineries in Russia and Europe, and some of the world’s largest independent traders of oil and petroleum pr oducts, with global presence, we source and supply Jet Fuel JP 54, Jet Fuel A-1, Aviation Kerosene Ts -1, D2 DIESEL GAS OIL, DIESEL GAS OIL (VIRGINE D6) EN590, Fuel Oil, LNG and LPG to our customers around the world. We are growing our active presence in the West African oil market.

Fertilizers

Specialised, Standard and Mono Fertilisers

Our company, working with major Fertilizer Manufacturers in the Baltic region, source and deliver cost effectively, a wide range of standard and mono fertilizers. These include Urea, Ammonium Nitrate (AN), Ammonium Sulphate (AS), Ammonium Sulphate Nitrate (ASN), Calcium Ammonium Nitrate(CAN), Muriate of Potash (MOP), Potassium Sulphate (SOP), Monoammonium Phosphate (MAP), Diammonium Phosphate (DAP), NPK 15-15-15, and NPK 16-16-16.
