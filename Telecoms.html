Telecommunication and Innovation

Mobile Phones

We are involved in original design, ODM manufacture, assembling of “ZONIC” brand Mobile Phones in collaboration with our Partners (IMG Technology Group); an internationally recognized mobile phone developer and ODM manufacturer with core competitiveness in OEM, ODM, OBM, and certified with SGS ISO9001: 2008 Quality Management Systems. We also handle design and manufacture of mobile phone devices for mobile phone operators and other clients.

Our Group is focussed on innovation and high tech solutions to problems facing mobile phone users in sub-Saharan African market, to address the needs of the market and phone users, and provide more efficient service delivery and customer care. “ZONIC” mobile phones are solution-driven, locally focused with global quality and European Union (EU) certification.

The official launch of “ZONIC” Flagship Mobile Smart and Feature Phones is being planned for the second quarter of 2019. More information about “ZONIC” Mobile Phones can be found on www.zonicimgmobile.com

Voice and Video Chat Application

Ariamson in partnership with Beta Mobile and other third party solutions provides free video and voice chat application on Android, IOS and Windows, and Tablets with innovative features including international voice and video calls, videoconference, and photo sharing contact album for users.

Smart Wearables and Mobile Accessories

In partnership with our Manufacturing Partners (Riversong); one of the world famous Smart Wearable and Mobile Accessories manufacture, we distribute offline, Smart Wearable and Mobile Accessories. Smart Wearables include Fitness Band (with functions including Blood Pressure and Heart Rate Monitor) Smart Watch, and Kid’s Watch. Audio products include Wired Earphone, Wireless Headphone, True Wireless and Speakers. Power category includes Power Bank, Wireless Charger and Car Charger. Cable products include Charging Cable and Hub. Riversong’s high quality Accessories and Smart Wearables are designed in Canada and produced in China and are sold world-wide by major online stores including Amazon.
