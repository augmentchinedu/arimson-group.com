What We Do

We have been connecting customers to the global economy for a quarter of a century. We grow prosperity by advancing trade.

We source, store, blend and deliver physical commodities reliably, efficiently and responsibly anywhere in the world. We add value to the global trade in natural resources with exceptional service and performance across the supply chain. We strengthen market links between producers and end-users and supply customers with the commodities they require when and where they need them.

Vision

To be a foremost conglomerate, delivering innovative solutions and services to meet the needs of customers.

Core Value

Ariamson’s five guiding principles are: professionalism, integrity, excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Innovation for Solution

DEVELOP: We develop solutions for mobile devices and mobile applications. In doing this we work with third party service providers, App developers and mobile phone manufacturers.

SOURCE: We negotiate and execute agreements with independent oil traders, national oil companies, miners, co-operatives, manufacturers to source commodities, minerals, industrial products, smart wearable and mobile accessories

ADVICE: We provide policy and management consulting services, advisory, transaction, funding, training and business support services.

DELIVER: We deliver mobile phones for sub-Sahara Africa and worldwide mobile market. Also, we provide new functionalities and improved features to mobile voice and video applications. We deliver quality consulting and advisory services, supply commodities, minerals, and other products to customers. We distribute and sale smart wearables and mobile phone accessories.

Management Team

Dr. Anthony Maduka, [PhD, FCMI] Executive Chairman & CEO

Dr. Maduka is a pioneer Director and Founder of Ariamson Group. He is an innovative, skilled strategist, result-oriented, ambitious, and conscientious Entrepreneur and Senior Executive Manager with 28 years’ management and leadership experience in public and private sectors, including 12 years’ at the strategic and Board-level from an international perspective.

He graduated Bachelor of Science (BSc) Hons degree in Business Administration (Second Class Upper) from the University of Benin Nigeria, Master of Science (MSc) degree in Management, and Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) degree in Management both from London South Bank University. Anthony is also a Fellow of Chartered Management Institute and has undergone one year of a two-year (LLM) Masters in Law (Qualifying Law Degree) programme at the University of London. He has attended many management and leadership programmes, and presented many management papers at many conferences across Europe, USA and Africa.

Anthony has held several management and leadership positions in both private and public organisations including Deputy Head of Scrutiny and Overview at London Government, Principal Consultant at Prima Consults United Kingdom, Managing Partner at Beaton Consulting, and CEO at Beaton Group, United Kingdom. Hezonic Limited for which Anthony is the Pioneer Director and CEO, in collaboration with China Netcom Group Corporation, Beijing China (now China Unicom) emerged as preferred bidder for Port Harcourt Zone of the $350m National Rural Telephony Project in Nigeria (NRTP) under the Federal Government of Nigeria Private-Public Partnership (PPP) arrangement. Also, Hezonic was member of New Generation Consortium, the erstwhile preferred Privatisation Bidder of $2.5billion for Nigeria Telecommunications Limited (NITEL) in Nigeria.

Dr. Maduka is financially astute, with a proven ability to set and monitor large budgets and analyse financial data to inform policy development and decision making. He negotiates effectively at all levels, building robust relationships with colleagues, stakeholders, and government authorities, and has developed and implemented effective business strategies that delivered growth, improvement and cost efficiency.

He is recognized as a decisive and motivational leader who is able to recruit, develop and retain cohesive teams focused on achieving organisational objectives. Confident managing high profile change projects, aligning the workforce with key business strategies and implementing coherent systems and processes. He combines international experience with natural aptitude for innovation and solutions for improved services and organisational success.

Anthony brings his 12 years’ Board-level experience, his extensive commercial acumen and operational experience, combined with senior leadership skills and proven ability to deliver results and change in the affairs of the Board. He speaks fluent German and is married with three children.

Marcellius Nwonu (Bsc, MSc, MBA, LLM, ACCA) Director (Finance & Administration)

Marcel has a multi-disciplinary capacity in economics, business administration, finance, accounting, law, audit and consultancy, and is a great asset to the Team. He graduated with Bachelor of Science (BSc) degree in Economics, specializing in Econometrics and Quantitative Analysis, and Masters of Science (MSc) degree in Economics, with specialization in Project Analysis and Evaluation, both from the University of Lagos, Lagos-Nigeria.
